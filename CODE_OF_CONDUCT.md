# Code Of Conduct

## Our Standard

Please be respectful, constructive, and kind.

Examples of positive behavior include:

- assuming good intent
- giving actionable feedback
- welcoming different experience levels
- focusing criticism on ideas and implementations, not people

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- harassment or personal attacks
- discriminatory or demeaning language
- sustained hostility or intimidation
- publishing private information without permission

## Scope

This applies to project discussions, issues, pull requests, and other community spaces connected to this repository.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and comments that violate this code of conduct.

If you run into a problem, contact the maintainers through the repository's issue tracker or the release/discussion channel you were invited through.
